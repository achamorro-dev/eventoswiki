## Netcoreconf Barcelona 2025

Discover the future of technology at Netcoreconf Barcelona 2025, where innovation meets the vibrant and creative spirit of a cosmopolitan city. Known for its iconic architecture and Mediterranean charm, Barcelona will set the stage for exploring the latest trends in **.NET, Azure, AI, DevOps, and Big Data**.

### World Class Speakers

We will have the largest international specialists to tell you all the technological news related to DotNet, Azure, AI, Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Modern Software Development Trends

### Best Experience

An event where you get to know the new cutting-edge technologies and create strategic links that generate joint synergies between professionals in the sector, companies and institutions.

### Networking

Throughout the event, you will have the opportunity to network promoted by both the speakers, attendees and collaborating companies.

### Exclusive Insights

Gain access to exclusive insider information, upcoming trends, and strategies directly from leaders shaping the future of technology.

### Hands-On Workshops

Engage in practical, hands-on sessions led by industry experts to deepen your knowledge and sharpen your skills in the latest technologies.

### Interactive Sessions

Participate in interactive sessions where you can ask top-tier speakers and industry specialists your most pressing questions.
