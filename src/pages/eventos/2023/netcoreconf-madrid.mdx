## Why you should Join Event

### World Class Speakers

We will have the largest international specialists to tell you all the technological news related to NetCore, Azure, Xamarin, IA, Big Data.

### Best Experience

An event where you get to know the new cutting-edge technologies and create strategic links that generate joint synergies between professionals in the sector, companies and institutions.

### Networking

Throughout the event, you will have the opportunity to network promoted by both the speakers, attendees and collaborating companies.

### Live in Twich

We will be live through our Twitch channel with two Tracks where you can interact directly with the speakers during the sessions.

### Videos on demand

All sessions will be available on demand through our YouTube channel permanently.

### Virtual Assistants Room

In this edition we have prepared special rooms through Discord so that attendees and speakers can interact "in a closer way".
